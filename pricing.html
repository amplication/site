We're in public beta and free to use

Amplication is an open-source project built by developers for developers. Our main objective is to provide a free lifetime "community developer license" to every developer around the world.

Public Beta users

While we're in Beta Amplication is free to use. In the future, we will offer a free community plan and additional paid business plans.

Although Amplication is currently in Beta, your generated apps are production-ready. Every app generated using Amplication platform contains stable, documented, secured, and supported production-ready open-source components & packages. Hosting

Each user account gets a free sandbox deployment environment for every app built with Amplication. The app sandbox has limited resources and can reset at any time, please do not use it for anything else other than development and testing. Remember, you can always export your code or your Docker container and deploy it elsewhere for production purposes. In the future, we will offer paid hosting services.
